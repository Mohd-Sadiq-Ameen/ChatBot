Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Built for people who want to be healthier without obsessing over numbers. Smart defaults, honest estimates, real AI advice.

🧠
AI Calorie Estimation
Just type what you ate. Select a portion size — small plate, medium glass, handful — and our AI estimates the calories instantly. No lookup tables.
📊
Honest Accuracy Ranges
Every estimate comes with a min–max range and a confidence score. We're upfront about uncertainty — homemade food varies, and we tell you that.
🤖
Personal AI Coach
Ask anything. Your coach knows your BMR, TDEE, today's intake and your goal. It gives specific, personalized advice — not generic tips.
⚡
BMR & TDEE Calculator
Set your weight, height, age, activity level and goal. We calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate and Total Daily Energy Expenditure automatically.
🥗
Macro Breakdown
Every food estimate includes protein, carbs and fat in grams. Understand not just calories but the quality of what you're eating.
🔥
Exercise Logging
Log calories burned from exercise. Your net intake and remaining budget update in real time, keeping your daily picture accurate.
